2130. Earth is dead.

Can you revive it?

It's the future. Humanity has irresponsibly depleted most of the Earth's resources, and after the global ecosystem collapsed, Earth became a hostile, inhospitable place. In the ensuing chaos, famine and conflict, most people either escaped to space or were left to die along with the planet.

Generations later, space-dwelling nations decide to attempt a last unified effort to make Earth a habitable planet again - the Environmental Restoration Agency.

Each region is assigned a single agent to oversee and guide the restoration. It is up to them to explore, prospect, cultivate, clean up and reintroduce greenery in what will come to be known as the largest life saving operation in the history of mankind.

Explore a simulated environment

The gameplay centers around surviving the hostile environment, and slowly restoring it using plants, machines and careful terraformation. You will face an unbreathable atmosphere, aggressive robotic enemies, and a lack of all resources necessary for life.

All of these elements are fully simulated - the atmosphere reacts to each tree you plant, the water flows and soaks into the ground. Your own character can only breathe in the areas you restored, and the enemies will avoid those areas.
